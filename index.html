Kickback Program

Unique Concept in the Netherlands

FlexPlekken IQ offers a revolutionary approach to coworking: become both a member and supporter of our workspaces and receive significant kickbacks on your membership.

How It Works

  • Base cost per month: €2,200
  • Revenue above this threshold is shared with members
  • Members receive 50% of the excess amount
  • Distribution is calculated monthly

Example Calculation

Monthly Revenue: €3,300
Base Cost: €2,200
